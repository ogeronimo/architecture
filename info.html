/biography

An Architect in training graduated in 2014 from the Polytechnic University of P.R.

For years my passion has been studying how art and design affect our environment. Ever since I knew that people's surroundings have a significant impact on their quality of life, I’ve been interested in architecture as a medium to interact with society and make sure it's a positive one. I’ve been fortunate to work with Architectural and Construction companies around the US and the Caribbean. The experience helped me increase my knowledge and abilities to take a project from concept to completion. My Specialties are in Design, Real Estate Development, Computer Software, Computer Drafting & Top Level Architectural Visualization.

I describe myself as an adventurer and thrill seeker, who believes in turning ideas into reality. I look forward to meeting you and find out how I can contribute to your business.
